Its is with great excitement that I have been chosen to be a vExpert for 2019. I can’t explain how exciting this is for me. The vExpert certification has been something I have been going after for about 3 years since I started working with VMware and I am glad to finally be apart of it.<\/p>\n

So what is vExpert?<\/p>\n

The vExpert program is a community evangelist certification that rewards community members that use, talk and promote VMware products.<\/p>\n

As part of the vExpert program each member gains access to the following:<\/p>\n

    \n
  • Private slack channel<\/li>\n
  • Private forum on VMware communities<\/li>\n
  • Access to private beta programs<\/li>\n
  • Pre-launch briefings on new VMware products<\/li>\n
  • Access to 365-day license keys for evaluation\/testing purpose<\/li>\n
  • and much much more.<\/li>\n<\/ul>\n

    I am most excited about the license keys. I have been wanting to set up both NSX and Horizon in the lab to start learning about and playing with micro-segmentation.<\/p>\n

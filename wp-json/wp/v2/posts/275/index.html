Recently, I had to migrate my VMNIC bindings from my Distributed Switches to my Standard Switches. Since my vCenter instance was being restored from backup (Failed 7.0 Update), I had to revert back to the ESXCLI.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

Step 1: Validate Current vDS Switch Settings<\/p>\n\n\n\n

esxcli network vSwitch dvs vmware list<\/pre>\n\n\n\n
Step 2: Remove NIC from vDS<\/p>\n\n\n\n
esxcfg-vswitch -Q vmnic_Name<\/em><\/strong> -V PortID_of_dvSwitch<\/strong><\/em> dvSwitch_Nam<\/strong>e<\/em><\/pre>\n\n\n\n
Step 3: Verify VMNIC was removed from vDS<\/p>\n\n\n\n
esxcli network vSwitch dvs vmware list<\/pre>\n\n\n\n
Step 4: Add VMNIC to vSS Switch (Can also be done by GUI but that is no fun)<\/p>\n\n\n\n
esxcli network vSwitch standard uplink add --uplink-name=vmnic<\/em>_Name<\/strong>\u00a0--vswitch-name=vSS_Switch<\/em>_Name<\/strong><\/pre>\n\n\n\n
